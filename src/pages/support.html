❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to your trip detail page, then click "Cancel Trip" at the bottom of the page.

Can I change my trip dates?

Yes, click "Edit Trip" on your trip page and update your travel dates.

How to get a travel receipt?

Receipts are available under each booking in your Trip History section.
